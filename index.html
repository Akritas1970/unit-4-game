Welcome to the Crystals Guessing Game

This is a simple game, where you try to match the number that is given to you at the beginning of the game.

Let's Play!

Instructions

  • Each crystal is assigned a random number at the start of the game.
  • When you click on a crystal its value is revealed to you and added to your score
  • You win when your score matches the number given to you at the start of the game
  • If you go over the number you lose
  • The game restarts when you win or lose and a new number is displayed and the crystals are assigned new values. Good luck!
